    JR Nagoya Station (JR Tokaido Honsen Line) - JR Gifu Station (Rapid, approx. 20 min)
    Meitetsu Nagoya Station (Meitetsu Nagoya Honsen Line) - Meitetsu Gifu Station (Limited Express, approx. 30 min)

    JR Gifu Station North Exit Bus Terminal No. 12 or 13 - “Nagarabashi” bus stop (approx. 20 minutes) - walk (approx. 5 minutes)
